About Us

A Unified Platform For "Campus to Corporate"

Platform For

We are .in startup on a mission to introduce amazing opportunities to students to land their dream jobs in corporate. Hiremi always believes in " Learning By Doing " We are doing a role as a talent acquisition partner for corporate and helping them with providing the right talent as well as we provided one platform for students where they can obtain information about available internships / Live Projects / Jobs / Trainings.

Hiremi is a platform that provides hiring-related services and shortlisted resumes for corporates/recruiters as well as a platform for corporations to publish their opportunities on our platform. Along with that for students, Hiremi provides :

  • Live Projects Updates
